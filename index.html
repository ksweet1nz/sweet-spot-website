The problem

You didn't build a mess on purpose.

  • You added tools one at a time. Each one solved a real problem. But now nothing connects to anything else. Your team works around the gaps. Data lives in spreadsheets. Growth has stalled and the ceiling is your own processes.

  • Your IT bill keeps rising. But nothing gets better. Nobody has ever audited what you are paying for, questioned a renewal, or asked whether a single tool is earning its place.

  • Most IT contractors understand the tools but not your business. Most consultants understand your business but hand you a document and leave. You are caught between the two.

  • Real operational complexity. No internal IT department. A stack that nobody fully understands.

The numbers

The numbers are straightforward.

30%+ of SaaS spend wasted

Tools that are underused or not used at all. The licence renews. Nobody notices.

50% of licences unused

Half of what your business pays for is sitting idle. Most businesses have never audited it.

4-7% of revenue

What businesses your size spend on technology every year. For a $3M to $5M business that is $120,000 to $350,000 annually.

The process

Fixed term. Three months. Four phases. No dependency at the end.

01

Audit

Weeks 1-2

You get a clear picture of your technology for the first time. Every tool, every cost, every gap. You will know exactly what you have, what it is doing, and what to fix.

02

Prioritise

Weeks 2-3

You decide what matters most. What gets fixed. What gets cut. What gets built. Nothing starts until you are confident in the direction.

03

Build

Weeks 3-11

Your systems get connected. Manual processes get automated. Tools that are not earning their place get retired. Your team gets trained on everything that is built - as it is built.

04

Hand over

Week 12

Everything documented. Your team ready. You walk away owning your technology - with no need to call anyone to keep it running.
