The problem

Your technology is costing more than it should.

  • You have accumulated software tools one at a time, for good reasons, until nothing connects to anything else. Growth has stalled and the old processes are the ceiling.

  • Your IT bill keeps rising with nothing to show for it. No one has ever audited the stack, questioned a renewal, or asked whether what you are paying for is earning its place.

  • Most IT contractors understand the tools but not the business. Most consultants understand the business but deliver a document. You need someone who does both.

  • You have real operational complexity but no internal IT department. New leadership, inherited mess, something is breaking.

The numbers

The numbers are straightforward.

4–7% of revenue

What small businesses typically spend on IT each year. For a $3M–$5M business, that is $120,000–$350,000 annually.

30%+ wasted

Of SaaS spend goes to underused or unused tools. 50% of software licences go unused entirely.

$0 ongoing retainer

You pay once. What gets built is yours. The savings it generates keep coming every year after that with no further cost.

The process

How it works

Fixed term. Three months. Four phases.

01

Audit

Weeks 1–2

What you have, what it costs, what it does and does not do. You leave this phase with a clear picture of your technology for the first time and a list of exactly what to fix.

02

Prioritise

Weeks 2–3

Agree on outcomes. What gets fixed, what gets cut, what gets built. You decide what matters most. Nothing starts until you are confident in the direction.

03

Build

Weeks 3–11

Your systems get connected. Manual processes get automated. The tools that are not earning their place get retired. Your team gets trained on everything that is built.

04

Hand over

Week 12

Everything documented. Your team trained. You walk away owning your technology with no need to call anyone to keep it running.
